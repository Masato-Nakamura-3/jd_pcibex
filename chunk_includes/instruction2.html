Instruction 2

When giving a continuation, please answer as clearly as possible in a loud voice so that your answer can be identified easily. Imagine you are talking to somebody 15 feet away form you!

Please make sure that no sound other than your answer is recorded. This is not just about background noise, but also filler words such as "umm" or "well...".

Please make sure to produce a word that comes to your mind first. Don't stop in the middle of your speech to change what you say.
